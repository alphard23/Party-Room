How to Use

Have you ever been to a party where the music sucks? Have you ever been a host, and have no idea what to play? Now you can have everyone participate in the music selection of the party!


First search or create your party room. Then look up songs that you want to play. You can also see everyones request and vote for which songs you want to hear first. The most voted songs will always play first, so the crappy music will have the lowest priority. Never hear terrible music again!
